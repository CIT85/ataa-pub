What This Site Is About

This website is dedicated to Beşiktaş, one of Turkey’s most historic and beloved football clubs. It explores the team’s identity, culture, rivalries, controversies, and passionate fan base. The goal is to go beyond scores and trophies, and instead spotlight the spirit of the club and its supporters.

Who It’s For

This site is for football fans, culture lovers, and anyone curious about the deeper stories behind Beşiktaş. Whether you're a longtime supporter or just discovering Turkish football, the content is designed to be both informative and engaging.

Why I Created It

As a lifelong Beşiktaş fan and a digital marketing student, I wanted to merge my love for the club with my growing skills in web development. This project gave me a way to creatively express that connection while learning modern HTML and CSS techniques.

Future Plans

In the future, I’d like to expand this site with interactive elements, match highlights, fan interviews, and even live data integration from league APIs. I also hope to improve the design with animations, dark mode, and mobile-first enhancements.
