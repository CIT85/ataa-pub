Controversial Calls

VAR or Nah?

Beşiktaş fans have long accused the Turkish Football Federation (TFF) and referees of inconsistent decision-making — especially in high-stakes matches. Several controversial VAR decisions have led to public statements from the club and fan protests.

  • 2022: Missed red card in Galatasaray derby
  • 2023: Offside call reversal vs Başakşehir
  • 2024: Questionable penalty given against Beşiktaş in the Cup semi final

Federation Tensions

Statements, Fines & Suspensions

Club officials have openly criticized the TFF over fairness and transparency. These statements often result in fines or bans, which further fuels fan frustration. There's a sense among many supporters that Beşiktaş is held to a different standard

2021-2024:
Over 500,000 TL in club fines related to press statements.
Referee suspensions:
Several top referees were removed mid-season due to consistent backlash from clubs — including Beşiktaş.

Media Pressure & Fan Response

Çarşi Speaks Loudly

Beyond the club, Beşiktaş's fan group Çarşi has taken a leading role in speaking out against injustices in the league. From creative tifos to banner protests, their voice has become central to the club's identity and defiance.

Social media hashtags like #Adaletİstiyoruz ("We want justice") often trend in Turkey after controversial losses.
